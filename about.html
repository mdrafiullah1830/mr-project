Our Story

🌱

Started with a Dream

MR Shop was born from a simple idea: bring authentic Bangladeshi products to everyone. From Sundarbans honey to Jamdani sarees, we wanted to celebrate our heritage through commerce.

🤝

Supporting Local Artisans

We work directly with local farmers, artisans, and small businesses. Every purchase supports a Bangladeshi family and helps preserve our traditional crafts.

🚀

Growing Every Day

From a small startup to Bangladesh's trusted marketplace, we've grown by putting customers first. Our 10,000+ happy customers are our biggest achievement.

Our Journey

2024

The Beginning

MR Shop was founded with a vision to promote Bangladeshi products globally. Started with just 10 products from local artisans.

2025

Growing Fast

Expanded to 200+ products across 6 categories. Launched seller platform and reached 5,000 customers.

2026

Today

500+ products, 10,000+ customers, 64 districts. Introducing AI-powered shopping and mobile app coming soon.

Our Mission & Values

01

Authenticity First

Every product on MR Shop is verified for authenticity. We guarantee genuine Bangladeshi products — from Sundarbans honey to Bogura doi.

02

Support Local

We empower local artisans, farmers, and small businesses. By selling on MR Shop, they reach customers across Bangladesh and beyond.

03

Customer Trust

100% secure payments, 7-day returns, and dedicated support. We build trust through transparency and reliability.

04

Social Impact

10% of our profits go to help underprivileged children. Every purchase you make creates a positive impact in someone's life.
