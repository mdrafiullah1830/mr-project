About Our Shop

Artisan BD is a family-run business dedicated to bringing authentic Bangladeshi products to your doorstep. We specialize in handpicked Sundarbans honey, traditional Nakshi Kantha quilts, pure dairy products, and artisan-crafted clothing. Every product is sourced directly from local farmers and artisans, ensuring fair trade and premium quality. With over 3 years on MR Shop, we have served more than 8,000 happy customers across all 64 districts of Bangladesh.

Store Policies

Shipping Policy
Free shipping on orders over ৳5,000 within Dhaka. Standard delivery takes 2-3 business days. Outside Dhaka: 3-5 business days. Express delivery available for ৳150 extra.
Return Policy
7-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Food items are non-returnable unless damaged. Handicrafts can be returned if defective. Contact seller within 48 hours of delivery.
Refund Policy
Full refund within 3 business days of approved return. Refund via bKash/Nagad or original payment method. Partial refunds may apply for used or damaged items.
Warranty
30-day warranty on all manufactured goods. Handicrafts come with a 90-day quality guarantee. Food products guaranteed fresh until expiry date. Free replacement for defective items.
Payment Methods
We accept Cash on Delivery, bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and bank transfers. All digital payments are processed through secure channels. 100% buyer protection guaranteed.
